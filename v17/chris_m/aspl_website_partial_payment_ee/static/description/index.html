Odoo 17 Partial Payment From Website (Enterprise)

This module allows Customer to do the Partial Payment from website.

Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo Enterprise edition but not tested in Community version. And we are supporting Ubuntu OS and not Windows OS.

Available for Community

Features

  • Partial Payment from website allow based on Partner.
  • Partial Payment from website side when customer purchase any item.
  • Partial Payment from website side Inside My Account Invoice.

How to use

  • Inside the Website configuration, you can configure the option like Minimum Order Amount, Advance Payment Amount, Minimum Payment Term and Maximum Partial Payment based on this configuration Partial Payment will work.
  • Inside the Customer Enable Partial Payment.
  • Inside the Customer, also you can configure the same option which you configured into the website configurations, but first it will consider the customer configuration if inside the customer configuration is not defined then it will consider the Website configuration.
  • Click on Partial Payment toggle button on website then one Textbox is visible and you can fill the amount.
  • When you Click on Pay Now Button then it will redirect on payment page.
  • Go to the My Account and click on Sales Orders you can see your confirmed order.
  • Go to the My Account and click on Invoices and Bills you can see your partial payment Invoices.
  • In Invoice Click on Pay Now button will open payment wizard Fill the Amount in Textbox which you want to pay.
  • After paid the Order successfully it will redirect to the Invoices and Bills page of My Account.
  • When you click on Process checkout it will create quotation of your order.
  • In My Account > Quotations you will see your order
  • When you select your Quotation you will see your Order details
  • click on sign and pay button will redirect you to Validate Order wizard where you can sign and then click on accept sign will redirect you to payment wizard.
  • In Payment wizard you can select partial payment after that text box is visible you can enter amount which you want to pay and click on pay
  • After paid partial amount it will redirect you to My Account > Sales Orders page
  • Your order related invoice you can see in My Account > Invoices and Bills menu
  • From invoice you can Py remaining amount.
  • Click on Pay Now button will redirect you to Payment wizard. From where you can pay partial or full amount and click on payment.
  • Payment button will redirect you to My Account > Invoices and Bills
  • If you paid all remaining amount you can see paid invoice otherwise same flow follow for due payment.
